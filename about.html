Welcome to PATEL OPTICALS

PATEL OPTICALS is an independently owned Optical Business, committed to providing quality Eyecare and Eyewear services in Georai (BEED).

With our qualified and experienced optometrists and knowledgeable dispensing opticians, our team is ready to look after the welfare of your eyes and vision.

We pride ourselves in providing individual solutions to fit in with your lifestyle, work and recreation needs. PATEL OPTICALS will provide you with thorough, professional and personalised vision care. Our aim is to do this in a relaxed, caring, and friendly environment and within a reasonable time frame. We promise to remain approachable, flexible and cost effective.

When you have your eyes examined at PATEL OPTICALS, you receive complete optometric care. Our optometrist and staff regularly upskill through continuing education and in-house training. With our up-to-date diagnostic and dispensing equipment, we combine our experience and knowledge to ensure a thorough and comprehensive eye examination.
